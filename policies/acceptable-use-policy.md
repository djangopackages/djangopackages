# DjangoPackages.org Acceptable Use Policy (AUP)

## Introduction

This Acceptable Use Policy (AUP) governs all use of DjangoPackages.org and its services. By accessing the site, users agree to abide by this AUP, ensuring that the platform remains a trusted and valuable resource for the Django community. 

## Acceptable Use

The services provided by DjangoPackages.org are designed to support the Django community by offering access to a wide range of packages and projects. Users of DjangoPackages.org are expected to use the services in a manner that is respectful, lawful, and in line with the following guidelines.

You may not use information from DjangoPackages.org (whether scraped, collected through our API, or obtained otherwise) for spamming purposes, including for the purposes of sending unsolicited emails to users or selling personal information, such as to recruiters, headhunters, and job boards.

### General Conduct

- **Respectful Communication:** Users must engage in respectful communication at all times, refraining from any form of harassment, bullying, or discrimination.

### Usage Restrictions

- **No Illegal Activity:** The services may not be used for any unlawful purposes or in furtherance of illegal activities.
- **No Malicious Software:** Uploading or distributing files that contain viruses, corrupted data, or any other malicious software is prohibited.
- **Resource Usage:** Users must not engage in activities that impair the operation of the site or the enjoyment of other users, such as overloading the system with automated requests.

### Content and Conduct

- **No Spam:** Posting spam, including excessive promotional material, junk mail, or unsolicited messages, is prohibited.
- **Responsible Disclosure:** If you discover a security vulnerability within DjangoPackages.org or any hosted package, please report it responsibly to the site administrators, avoiding public disclosure until the issue is addressed.

## Enforcement

Violations of this AUP may result in temporary or permanent suspension of access to DjangoPackages.org services. The administrators reserve the right to remove any content or suspend any account that, in their judgment, violates this AUP or may expose DjangoPackages.org or its users to harm or liability.

## Changes to the AUP

DjangoPackages.org may modify this AUP at any time. Continued use of the services after any such changes constitutes your consent to the changes.

## Reporting Violations

To report a violation of this AUP, please contact the DjangoPackages.org team through the designated reporting channels available on our website.
