Help

How do I add a package?

Go to the home page and click on the bright green button that says Add Package. Then paste in the URL of your repo. Open Comparison then tries to determine your package's repo name and PyPI information from the URL. Click submit and it will process your request.

How do I add a grid?

Go to the home page and click on the bright green button that says Add Grid. Add the mandatory title and slug, and an optional description.

Need more help?

  • Tweet at pydanny or Open Comparison itself.
  • Submit an issue.
  • Send an email to the team.
